Welcome to Hanbit Horizon Nepal.

We are a professional tour and trek company in Nepal. We specialize in customized trekking experiences, cultural tours, and adventure activities across Nepal. Our team consists of government-certified trekking guides with extensive knowledge of Nepal's mountains, culture, and traditions.

We maintain the highest safety standards with proper acclimatization schedules, emergency evacuation plans, and comprehensive insurance coverage.

  • Nepal Tourism Board registered agency
  • Eco-friendly trekking practices
  • Fair wages for all team members
  • Transparent pricing, no hidden costs

Our Services & Expertise

Comprehensive travel solutions designed to make your Nepal adventure seamless and memorable

Expert Local Guides

Our guides aren’t just workers—they are your mountain family. We know the trails, the people, and the secret viewpoints. We make sure you stay safe, feel comfortable, and have a lot of fun along the way.

  • Friendly, licensed local experts
  • Trained in mountain safety & first-aid
  • Great stories about our culture
  • We go at your pace (no rushing!)

Your Trip, Your Way

Every traveler is different, so every trip should be too. Tell us your dream, your budget, and how much time you have. We’ll build a custom plan just for you, so you get exactly the adventure you’re looking for.

  • Custom routes for your fitness level
  • Pick your own start and end dates
  • Solo travelers or private groups
  • Expert advice on the best seasons

Cultural & Heritage Tours

Nepal is more than just mountains. Let us show you the incredible history and traditions of our valley. From the "Monkey Temple" to the ancient palaces, our cultural tours give you a real look into the heart of Nepal.

  • Explore Kathmandu’s hidden temples
  • Visit UNESCO World Heritage sites
  • Private tours in Patan & Bhaktapur
  • Learn about our deep history

Buses & Private Cars

Getting around Nepal shouldn't be a headache. Whether you want a cheap tourist bus ticket or a private jeep for your group, we’ll book it for you. We only work with reliable drivers to keep your journey smooth and safe.

  • Daily tourist buses to Pokhara & Chitwan
  • Comfortable private cars & jeeps
  • Luxury buses with AC and WiFi
  • Easy airport pickups (no stress!)

Helicopter Tours

If you want a truly special experience (or you're short on time), nothing beats a helicopter flight. See the highest mountains in the world from above or fly directly to base camp for breakfast. It’s an adventure you’ll never forget.

  • Everest Base Camp breakfast flights
  • Quick luxury mountain views
  • Emergency rescue coordination
  • Annapurna & Langtang flights

Hotel & Resort Bookings

From cozy mountain lodges to 5-star luxury resorts in Kathmandu and Pokhara, we help you find the perfect place to rest. We personally visit our partner hotels to ensure they meet our high standards for cleanliness, service, and hospitality.

  • Budget to 5-star luxury options
  • Hand-picked, clean & safe stays
  • Best rates through local partnerships
  • Flexible cancellation on most rooms

Mountain Gear Rental

Don't have your own gear? No problem! You can rent top-quality jackets and sleeping bags from us. We also have amazing porters to carry your heavy loads, so you can walk light and enjoy the beautiful mountain views.

  • Rent warm sleeping bags & jackets
  • Strong, friendly porters for your bags
  • We handle all permits & paperwork
  • Help for independent travelers

24/7 Travel Support

We take care of all the "boring" stuff so you don't have to. From booking nice hotels in Kathmandu to arranging domestic flights and helping with your visa, we’re here to make your entire trip easy and stress-free.

  • Good hotels (Budget to Luxury)
  • Domestic flight tickets
  • Help with Visa extensions
  • Travel insurance advice
